Out of combat

Kenku Recall 2×/long rest

Before rolling, give yourself advantage on an ability check using a skill you're proficient in (Arcana, History, Investigation, Insight, Perception, Sleight of Hand).

Mimicry unlimited

Imitate any voice or sound you've heard. Detecting the fake needs an Insight check vs DC 9. Brutal with Minor Illusion.

Expert Forgery unlimited

Advantage to forge or duplicate handwriting, documents and objects.

Familiar (Find Familiar) ritual / 1×/rest

Your raven scouts, spies, and delivers your touch spells. See through its eyes as an action.

Rituals (Ritual Adept) 10 min

Cast Detect Magic (and any ritual in your book) without preparing it or spending a slot.

Magic utility unlimited

Prestidigitation and Mage Hand: countless minor tricks and handling objects at range.

Your strength: knowledge checks

Arcana, History and Investigation at +6. You're the party's brains.
